Ground Truth = According to chapter 6B of the Penal Code, A person commits genocide who, with intent to destroy, in whole or in part, a national, an ethnical, a racial or a religious group, commits any of the following acts:
(a)        killing members of the group;
(b)        causing serious bodily or mental harm to members of the group;
(c)        deliberately inflicting on the group conditions of life calculated to bring about its physical destruction in whole or in part;
(d)        imposing measures intended to prevent births within the group; or
(e)        forcibly transferring children of the group to another group. Whoever commits genocide shall —
(a)	if the offence consists of the killing of any person, be punished with death; or
(b)	in any other case, be punished with imprisonment for life or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 20 years.
